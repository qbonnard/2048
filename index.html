How to play:

  • Use Firefox or Chrome to share your webcam with the page's javascript.
  • Print or display on your phone the tag image at http://goo.gl/GWdcT2.
  • Show the tag image to make the game appear, and tilt it to move the tiles.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
